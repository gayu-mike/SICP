* Exercise 2.26

** Q
   #+BEGIN_SRC scheme
   (define x (list 1 2 3))
   (define y (list 4 5 6))
   #+END_SRC

*** A
    #+BEGIN_SRC scheme
    (append x y)
    ;; (1 (2 (3 (4 5 6 nil))))  ... evaluating
    ;; (1 2 3 4 5 6)

    (cons x y)
    ;; ((1 2 3 nil) (4 5 6 nil))  ... evaluating
    ;; ((1 2 3) 4 5 6)

    (list x y)
    ;; ((1 2 3 nil) (4 5 6 nil) nil)  ... evaluating
    ;; ((1 2 3) (4 5 6))
    #+END_SRC
